## What is StudyQuest?

StudyQuest is an AI-powered mobile study platform that transforms PDF documents into engaging, interactive learning experiences. Upload your study materials, and our AI creates personalized flashcards and quizzes to help you master any subject.

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload Your PDF">
    Select any PDF document containing your study material. This becomes your study "world."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="AI Generates Content">
    Our backend worker powered by Google Gemini analyzes your PDF and creates relevant flashcards and quiz questions.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Study with Flashcards">
    Review key concepts with interactive flashcards using spaced repetition to optimize retention.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Test Your Knowledge">
    Challenge yourself with AI-generated quizzes featuring multiple-choice questions and detailed explanations.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Track Your Progress">
    Earn XP, unlock new levels, and maintain your learning streak as you master each subject.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Technology Stack

StudyQuest is built with modern technologies for performance and scalability:

- **Frontend**: Flutter (Dart) for cross-platform mobile, web, and desktop
- **Backend**: Supabase for authentication, database, and storage
- **AI**: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash for content generation
- **Architecture**: Clean Architecture with BLoC state management
- **Features**: Text-to-speech, local notifications, PDF processing
