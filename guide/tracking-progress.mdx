StudyQuest gamifies your learning experience with multiple progress tracking systems to keep you motivated and engaged.

## Home Screen Stats

Your key statistics are always visible at the top of the Mis Mundos screen:

### Streak Counter

- **Icon**: 🔥 Fire emoji
- **Color**: Orange
- **Meaning**: Number of consecutive days you've studied
- **Location**: Top-right corner of the home screen

<Tip>
  Maintain your streak by completing at least one lesson every day. Even a quick flashcard session counts!
</Tip>

### XP (Experience Points)

- **Icon**: 💎 Diamond emoji  
- **Color**: Blue
- **Meaning**: Total experience points earned across all worlds
- **Location**: Top-right corner, next to streak

<Note>
  XP is earned by completing lessons:
  - Flashcard sessions: +10 XP
  - Quiz completions: +20 XP
</Note>

## Accessing Your Profile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Locate Profile Button">
    On the home screen (Mis Mundos), look for the circular profile icon in the top-right corner of the app bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Profile Icon">
    Tap the small circular avatar (person icon with gray background) to open your profile page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Your Stats">
    Your profile page displays detailed information about your learning journey.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile Page Overview

The profile page is divided into several sections:

### Header Section

- **Profile Avatar**: Large circular icon representing your account
- **Username**: Your display name (e.g., "Estudiante")
- **Study Level**: Shows your current level (e.g., "Nivel de Estudio: Principiante")

### Statistics Cards

Two prominent stat cards display your key metrics:

<Accordion title="Streak Card">
  - **Icon**: 🔥 Fire department symbol
  - **Color**: Orange theme
  - **Value**: Your current consecutive day streak
  - **Label**: "Racha"
  
  This card shows how many days in a row you've studied. Building a long streak is one of the best ways to form a lasting study habit.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="XP Card">
  - **Icon**: 💎 Diamond symbol
  - **Color**: Blue theme
  - **Value**: Your total experience points
  - **Label**: "Puntos XP"
  
  This card displays all XP you've accumulated from completing flashcard sessions and quizzes across all your worlds.
</Accordion>

### Achievements Section

Below your stat cards, you'll find "Logros y Medallas" (Achievements and Medals):

#### First Lesson Achievement

- **Icon**: ⭐ Star
- **Color**: Amber/Gold
- **Title**: "Primera Lección"
- **Status**: "Completado"
- **Unlocked**: After completing your very first lesson

#### Dedicated Student Achievement

- **Icon**: 🛡️ Shield
- **Color**: Indigo
- **Title**: "Estudiante Dedicado"
- **Status**: "Racha de 5 días"
- **Unlocked**: After maintaining a 5-day study streak

<Note>
  More achievements will be added as you continue your learning journey. Check back regularly to see what you've unlocked!
</Note>

## World Progress Tracking

### Individual World Cards

On the Mis Mundos screen, each world card shows:

- **World Title**: Name of your study material
- **Progress Bar**: Visual indicator of completion percentage
- **Completion Percentage**: Numeric display (e.g., "0%")

<Tip>
  The progress bar fills as you complete levels within that world. A full bar means you've mastered all the content!
</Tip>

### Level Map Progress

When you enter a world, the level map shows:

- **Completed Levels**: Normal colored circles with checkmarks
- **Current Level**: Highlighted, unlocked and available
- **Locked Levels**: Gray circles with lock icons
- **Final Goal**: Trophy/medal icon at the end of the path

## Understanding XP Rewards

Different activities award different amounts of XP:

| Activity | XP Reward | Duration |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| Complete Flashcard Session | +10 XP | 5-10 minutes |
| Complete Quiz | +20 XP | 10-15 minutes |
| Daily Login Streak | Bonus multiplier | Daily |

<Warning>
  You only earn XP for completing full sessions. Partial completions (like running out of lives in a quiz) don't award points.
</Warning>

## Maintaining Your Streak

### How Streaks Work

Your streak increases by 1 each day you complete at least one learning activity:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Day 1: Start Your Streak">
    Complete any flashcard session or quiz to start a new streak.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Day 2-4: Build Momentum">
    Return each day to complete at least one activity. Your streak counter increases.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Day 5: Unlock Achievement">
    Hit 5 days to unlock the "Estudiante Dedicado" achievement!
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Keep Going">
    Continue daily to build longer streaks and form a lasting habit.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Streak Tips

<Accordion title="Set a Daily Reminder">
  Choose a specific time each day for studying. Consistency makes it easier to maintain your streak.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Start Small">
  Even a single flashcard session counts. On busy days, do a quick 5-minute review to keep your streak alive.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Don't Break the Chain">
  Losing a streak can be demotivating. If you're close to midnight, even a quick quiz question is worth it!
</Accordion>

## Refreshing Your Data

To see the latest progress updates:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Pull to Refresh on Home Screen">
    On the Mis Mundos screen, pull down on the world list to refresh all data.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Navigate Away and Back">
    Opening and closing screens automatically syncs your latest XP and streak data.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Profile Page">
    Visit your profile page to see the most current statistics and achievements.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Signing Out

<Warning>
  Before signing out, make sure all your progress has synced to the cloud. Look for updated XP and streak numbers.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Profile Page">
    Tap the profile icon on the home screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Logout Icon">
    In the top-right corner of the profile page, tap the red logout icon (🚪).
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Sign Out">
    A dialog asks "¿Estás seguro de que deseas salir de tu cuenta?"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Action">
    - Tap "Cancelar" to stay logged in
    - Tap "Salir" to log out and return to the welcome screen
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your progress is saved to your account. When you log back in, all your worlds, XP, streaks, and achievements will be restored.
</Note>

## Progress Milestones

As you use StudyQuest, aim for these milestones:

- ✅ Complete your first flashcard session
- ✅ Pass your first quiz without losing lives
- ✅ Reach a 5-day study streak
- ✅ Earn 100 total XP
- ✅ Complete an entire world (all levels)
- ✅ Maintain a 30-day streak
- ✅ Create worlds for multiple subjects

<Tip>
  Set personal goals beyond what the app tracks. For example, aim to complete one world per week or earn 50 XP daily.
</Tip>

## Interpreting Your Stats

### High XP, Low Streak

You're doing lots of studying but not consistently every day. Try scheduling regular, shorter sessions instead of irregular cramming.

### High Streak, Low XP

You're consistent but might be doing minimal work each day. Challenge yourself to complete more lessons per session.

### Balanced Growth

If both XP and streak are growing steadily, you've found an excellent sustainable study rhythm. Keep it up!
