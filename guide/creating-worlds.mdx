---
title: Creating Worlds
description: Learn how to create your first study world by uploading PDF documents
---

StudyQuest transforms your PDF study materials into interactive learning worlds. Each world contains automatically generated lessons, flashcards, and quizzes based on your document content.

## Uploading Your First PDF

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Mis Mundos">
    From the home screen, you'll see your worlds dashboard. This is where all your study worlds are displayed.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap the New World Button">
    Look for the floating action button labeled "Nuevo Mundo PDF" at the bottom of the screen. This button opens your device's file picker.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your PDF">
    Choose a PDF file from your device. Make sure it contains text content (not just images) for best results.
    
    <Note>
      Only PDF files are currently supported. The file should be a study material, textbook chapter, or any educational document you want to learn from.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Wait for Processing">
    After selecting your file, StudyQuest's AI will begin creating your world. You'll see a processing indicator with the message "Creando lecciones..." (Creating lessons).
    
    <Warning>
      Processing time varies based on document length. Larger PDFs may take several minutes. Don't close the app during this process.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Access Your World">
    Once processing is complete, tap on your world card to enter and start exploring the generated lessons.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding World Cards

Each world appears as a card on your home screen with the following information:

- **World Title**: The name of your PDF document
- **Progress Bar**: Shows your completion percentage for this world
- **Status Indicator**: Displays whether the world is ready or still processing

<Tip>
  World cards use a beautiful themed background pattern. Tap any ready world to enter its level map and start learning.
</Tip>

## Managing Your Worlds

### Deleting a World

If you want to remove a world and start fresh:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Long Press the World Card">
    Press and hold on the world you want to delete. This will open a confirmation dialog.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Read the warning carefully. Deleting a world removes all progress, XP, and levels associated with it.
    
    <Warning>
      This action cannot be undone. All your progress in that world will be permanently lost.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Eliminar">
    Confirm your choice by tapping the "Eliminar" button. The world will be removed from your dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Refreshing Your Worlds

Pull down on the world list to refresh and sync your latest progress. This is useful after completing lessons to see updated statistics.

## Best Practices

<Accordion title="Choose Quality Study Materials">
  Select well-formatted PDFs with clear text. Documents with complex layouts, heavy images, or scanned pages may not process as effectively.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Start with Shorter Documents">
  If you're new to StudyQuest, begin with shorter PDFs (10-20 pages) to get familiar with how the AI generates content.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Organize by Subject">
  Create separate worlds for different subjects or topics. This helps you focus your study sessions and track progress more effectively.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Name Your Files Clearly">
  The PDF filename becomes your world title, so use descriptive names like "Biology Chapter 3" instead of "document.pdf".
</Accordion>

## Troubleshooting

### World Stuck in Processing

If your world shows "Creando lecciones..." for an extended time:

1. Check your internet connection
2. Pull down to refresh the world list
3. Wait a few more minutes for large documents
4. If it persists beyond 10 minutes, try deleting and re-uploading

### Cannot Upload PDF

If the file picker doesn't accept your PDF:

- Ensure the file extension is `.pdf`
- Check that the file isn't corrupted
- Verify you have a stable internet connection
- Try uploading from a different location on your device

<Tip>
  Processing works in the background! You can navigate away from the worlds screen and return later to check if your world is ready.
</Tip>
