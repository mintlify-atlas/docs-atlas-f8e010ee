---
title: Taking Quizzes
description: Test your knowledge with interactive multiple-choice quizzes and earn rewards
---

Quizzes in StudyQuest challenge you to apply what you've learned. Unlike flashcards, quizzes test your ability to recognize and select correct answers under pressure.

## Starting a Quiz

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to a Quiz Level">
    From your world's level map, look for levels marked with a quiz icon (❓). These are quiz challenges.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Ensure Level is Unlocked">
    Quiz levels unlock sequentially. You must complete previous levels first.
    
    <Note>
      Tapping a locked level shows the message: "Completa el nivel anterior para desbloquear este."
    </Note>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap to Begin">
    Tap the unlocked quiz level to start your test. The quiz begins immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quiz Interface

### Lives System

Quizzes feature a **3-heart lives system** displayed in the top-right corner:

- **Full Hearts (♥)**: Indicates remaining lives
- **Empty Hearts (♡)**: Shows lives you've lost
- **Lose All Lives**: The quiz ends and you must retry

<Warning>
  Each incorrect answer costs you one life. If you run out of lives, the quiz ends immediately without XP rewards.
</Warning>

### Progress Tracking

At the top of the screen, you'll see:

- **Question Counter**: "Pregunta X / Y" shows your position
- **Progress Bar**: Visual indicator of quiz completion (teal color)
- **Lives Display**: Heart icons showing remaining attempts

## Answering Questions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Read the Question">
    Each question appears in a white card with a speaker icon. Tap the speaker icon to hear the question read aloud.
    
    <Tip>
      Use the audio feature if you're a better auditory learner or want to verify you understood the question correctly.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review All Options">
    Below the question, you'll see multiple choice options labeled A, B, C, etc. Read all options carefully before selecting.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Answer">
    Tap on the option you believe is correct. Once selected, you cannot change your answer.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="See Instant Feedback">
    The app immediately shows whether you're correct or incorrect with visual and audio cues.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Feedback and Results

### Correct Answers

When you answer correctly:

- **Green highlight**: The correct option turns green
- **Success sound**: A pleasant "correct" sound effect plays
- **Avatar celebration**: Your study companion shows a happy reaction
- **Explanation box**: Green panel appears with "¡Correcto!" message

### Incorrect Answers

When you answer incorrectly:

- **Red highlight**: Your selected wrong answer turns red
- **Green highlight**: The correct answer is highlighted in green
- **Error sound**: A gentle "wrong" sound effect plays
- **Life lost**: One heart disappears from the top bar
- **Avatar reaction**: Your study companion shows a disappointed expression
- **Explanation box**: Red panel appears with "Incorrecto" and helpful explanation

<Note>
  The explanation text helps you understand why the correct answer is right, even when you get it wrong. Read these carefully!
</Note>

## Interactive Avatar

Your study companion (ReactiveAvatar) provides emotional feedback:

- **Idle State**: Calm, waiting for you to answer
- **Success Reaction**: Celebrates when you answer correctly
- **Fail Reaction**: Shows concern when you make a mistake
- **Celebrate Reaction**: Throws a party when you complete the quiz

The avatar appears in a popup bubble with smooth scaling animations to make the experience more engaging.

## Audio Features

### Question Text-to-Speech

Tap the speaker icon (🔊) next to any question to hear it read aloud in Spanish. This helps with:

- Understanding complex questions
- Accessibility for visual impairments
- Reinforcing learning through multiple senses

### Sound Effects

- **Correct Answer**: Positive chime sound
- **Wrong Answer**: Gentle error tone

<Tip>
  Make sure your device volume is on to enjoy the full audio experience!
</Tip>

## Game Over Scenario

If you lose all three lives:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Game Over Dialog">
    A dialog appears with the message "¡Te quedaste sin vidas! 💔" and shows your avatar looking sad.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Encouragement Message">
    The app reminds you: "No te desanimes. Repasa los apuntes y vuelve a intentarlo."
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="End Attempt">
    Tap "Terminar Intento" to return to the level map. You can retry the quiz after reviewing your materials.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  No XP is awarded if you run out of lives. You must complete the quiz with at least one life remaining to earn rewards.
</Note>

## Completing a Quiz Successfully

When you answer all questions while maintaining at least one life:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Completion Dialog">
    A success dialog appears: "¡Examen Completado! 🎓"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Avatar Celebration">
    Your study companion performs a celebratory animation
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confetti Effect">
    Colorful confetti explodes across the screen
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="XP Reward">
    You receive **+20 XP** (double the flashcard reward) displayed in an orange badge
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Level Unlocked">
    The quiz is marked complete and the next level unlocks
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Claim Reward">
    Tap "Reclamar Recompensa" to return to the level map
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Quiz Strategy Tips

<Accordion title="Read All Options First">
  Don't rush to select the first answer that looks right. Read all options - sometimes multiple answers seem correct but only one is best.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Eliminate Obviously Wrong Answers">
  If you're unsure, mentally cross out options you know are incorrect. This improves your odds on challenging questions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Use the Audio Feature">
  Listen to questions read aloud to catch details you might miss when reading quickly.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Learn from Mistakes">
  When you get an answer wrong, read the explanation carefully before moving to the next question. Understanding why you were wrong is crucial.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Pace Yourself">
  You're not timed, so take your time to think through each question. Rushing leads to careless mistakes and lost lives.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Review Before Retrying">
  If you run out of lives, go back to the flashcard lessons to review concepts before attempting the quiz again.
</Accordion>

## Understanding Question Quality

Quiz questions are AI-generated from your study materials and include:

- **Factual recall questions**: Test your memory of key information
- **Comprehension questions**: Test your understanding of concepts
- **Application questions**: Test your ability to apply knowledge

<Warning>
  Question difficulty varies based on your source material's complexity. More advanced textbooks generate more challenging quizzes.
</Warning>

## Accessibility Features

- **Large touch targets**: Option cards are sized for easy tapping
- **High contrast colors**: Clear visual distinction between states
- **Audio support**: Text-to-speech for all questions
- **Clear feedback**: Visual and audio cues for every interaction

<Tip>
  Quizzes are great for testing retention before real exams. Try completing quiz levels multiple times to reinforce learning!
</Tip>
